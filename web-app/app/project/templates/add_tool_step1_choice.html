Choose a method for integrating your Clone detector Tool

Welcome to our web applicationâ€™s Clone Detector Tool integration process. We offer two distinct methods for adding your tool.

Upon successful integration using either of these methods, your clone detector tool will become an part of our web application, available for use in the clone detection benchmarking.
We invite you to select the method that best aligns with your requirements.

Integration via Docker Image

Designed for users who have already containerized their clone detector tool into a Docker image. It is the ideal choice for those familiar with Docker technology and who have their tool ready in a Docker format.

You will be prompted to provide the necessary Docker image details for direct integration into our web application.



Important:
Please ensure that your Clone Detector tool and Docker image meet our requirements: https://git.uni-jena.de/pi65gop/klondetektoren-automatisierung/-/blob/main/HOWTO_addTool.md Non-compliance may lead to failure in the integration process or during the execution of your tool. Your attention to these guidelines is crucial for a successful integration.

Integration via Git Repository

Aimed at users who may not be versed in Docker or prefer to avoid the containerization process, this method allows for the addition of your tool through a Git repository URL.

Our system will take care of the Docker image creation from your provided repository.



Important:
Please ensure that your Clone Detector tool and Git repository meet our requirements: https://git.uni-jena.de/pi65gop/klondetektoren-automatisierung/-/blob/main/HOWTO_addTool.md Non-compliance may lead to failure in the integration process or during the execution of your tool. Your attention to these guidelines is crucial for a successful integration.
